How ClipForge works
01
Paste any YouTube URL
Drop in any public YouTube video link. The longer the video, the more clips we can find — long-form content works best.
02
AI finds viral moments
Claude AI analyzes your video's title and structure to identify the highest-potential moments: surprising reveals, emotional peaks, quotable lines, hot takes.
03
yt-dlp downloads & ffmpeg trims
yt-dlp fetches the full video, then ffmpeg centre-crops it to 9:16 phone format and trims each clip with optional subtitle overlay.
04
Download & post
All clips are MP4 files ready for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. Download individually or grab them all at once.
